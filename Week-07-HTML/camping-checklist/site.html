Camping Checklist

Site Selection

  • Pitch your tent away from dead trees or trees with limbs that might fall in a storm
  • Find a site that has access to a water source
  • Pick a site large enough to contain your gear
  • Look for relatively even ground with a gentle pitch, in case of rain
  • Find shade in summer
  • Find open sky in winter
